How to Use

Getting Started

SpeedSpectrum Pro automatically runs a speed test when you open the page. You'll see your live download and upload speeds updating in real-time.

Country Selection

Use the dropdown to test your connection to different regions:

  • 🇳🇬 Nigeria — ACCURATE: Tests your real local speed to Nigerian CDN servers
  • 🌍 Auto — Uses the closest available server
  • 🇺🇸 USA / 🇬🇧 UK / etc. — SIMULATED: Shows approximate performance to that region
💡 Important: The tool does NOT connect to actual servers in other countries. Instead, it applies realistic latency factors to simulate international performance.

Features Explained

  • Live Speed Test — Shows download/upload speeds as they're measured
  • Quality Score — Rates your connection from "Excellent" to "Very Poor"
  • History Chart — Tracks your speeds over time to show trends
  • Export CSV — Download your test history for analysis or ISP reports
  • Auto-Test — Automatically re-tests every 60 seconds (toggle on/off)
  • Ping & Jitter — Measures latency and connection stability
  • Packet Loss — Shows the percentage of data packets lost
  • IP Address Display — Shows your public IP and ISP (no data stored)
  • Dark/Light Mode — Toggle between themes for comfortable viewing
  • Live History Updates — Every 10-second update is automatically saved to history
